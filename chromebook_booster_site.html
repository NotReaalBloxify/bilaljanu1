🌐 School Network Optimization

📡DNS Optimization for Schools

Best DNS servers for school networks:

School-Friendly DNS Servers:
• Cloudflare: 1.1.1.1 and 1.0.0.1 (Fastest)
• Google: 8.8.8.8 and 8.8.4.4 (Most Compatible)
• OpenDNS: 208.67.222.222 and 208.67.220.220 (School-Safe)
🏫 For School Networks:
Steps to change DNS:
  1. Click WiFi icon in bottom-right corner
  2. Select your school network and click the gear icon
  3. Expand "Network" section
  4. Change "Name servers" to "Custom name servers"
  5. Enter your preferred DNS servers
  6. Click "Apply" and reconnect
